TempShot Settings

Temporary screenshots for AI workflows — fast, private, local.

Keyboard shortcut

Press Alt+Shift+S on any normal page to capture the visible area. The screenshot is saved temporarily inside TempShot — nothing is downloaded to your desktop.

To change the shortcut — or bind an optional shortcut for full-page capture — open chrome://extensions/shortcuts. Chrome lets you pick any key combination there; extensions cannot change bindings for you.

Auto-delete

Screenshots are temporary by design. Choose how long they stick around.

Capture behavior

Privacy

TempShot is 100% local. Screenshots live in your browser's extension storage (IndexedDB) until deleted, auto-deleted, or exported by you. No uploads, no accounts, no analytics — the extension makes zero network requests.
