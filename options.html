TempShot Settings

Temporary screenshots for AI workflows — fast, private, local.

Keyboard shortcuts

Capture full page optional — also available as a button in the popup …

Chrome keeps all extension shortcuts in one protected editor (so no extension can silently take over your keys). The button opens it with TempShot listed — click a box, press the combo you want, done. Your choice shows up here automatically.

Auto-delete

Screenshots are temporary by design. Choose how long they stick around.

Capture behavior

Every capture shows a brief flash and a “saved” pill on the page, plus a ✓ on the toolbar icon — no sound, ever.

Privacy

TempShot is 100% local. Screenshots live in your browser's extension storage (IndexedDB) until deleted, auto-deleted, or exported by you. No uploads, no accounts, no analytics — the extension makes zero network requests.
